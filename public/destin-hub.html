Destin, Florida sits on the Emerald Coast — named for the emerald green Gulf of Mexico waters and sugar-white quartz sand beaches. It is consistently ranked among the best beach destinations in the United States. The area offers world-class fishing, watersports, seafood restaurants, and family activities within a short drive of each other.

🏖 Best Beaches in Destin

  • Henderson Beach State Park — pristine shoreline, nature trails, entry fee $4/vehicle. Worth every penny for the uncrowded stretch of beach.
  • Crystal Beach — calm, shallow waters ideal for families with young children. Free street parking available early morning.
  • James Lee Park — free parking, restrooms, lifeguards in season. Best for groups and families.
  • Pelican Beach Resort — private beach access for guests. No road to cross, elevator straight to the sand.
💡 Local Insight

Avoid the main boardwalk crowds — head to James Lee Park before 9 AM for free parking. Henderson Beach is worth the entry fee for the quiet and the nature trail.

🦞 Best Restaurants in Destin

  • AJ's Seafood & Oyster Bar — iconic HarborWalk spot, waterfront views, live music most nights.
  • Boshamps Seafood — best harbor sunset view in Destin. Fresh Gulf catches, great for groups.
  • Pompano Joe's — casual beachfront, great for lunch after the beach.
  • The Back Porch — oldest beach bar in Destin, open deck over the Gulf.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best beaches in Destin FL?

Henderson Beach State Park, Crystal Beach, and James Lee Park are the top picks. All feature white quartz sand and emerald green Gulf waters the Emerald Coast is known for.

What airport do you fly into for Destin Florida?

VPS (Destin-Fort Walton Beach) is closest at 15 minutes. ECP (Panama City Beach) is 45 minutes with more flight options. PNS (Pensacola) is 1 hour with the most airlines.

What is the best time to visit Destin Florida?

May through October for warm water and beach weather. June–August is peak season. Spring and fall offer lower prices with still-pleasant temperatures and fewer crowds.

Do you need a car in Destin Florida?

Yes — public transportation is limited. Most attractions are spread along US-98. Renting a car at VPS, ECP, or PNS is recommended.

What are the best restaurants in Destin Florida?

AJ's Seafood & Oyster Bar, Boshamps Seafood, Pompano Joe's, and The Back Porch. HarborWalk Village has numerous waterfront dining options within walking distance.

How do I find cheap flights to Destin Florida?

Compare fares to VPS, ECP, and PNS. Booking 6–8 weeks ahead and flying midweek yields the best prices. ECP and PNS typically have more competitive fares than VPS. Read our full airport guide →

Common Destin Travel Questions

What are the best seafood restaurants in Destin for 2026?

AJ's Seafood & Oyster Bar remains the most popular for waterfront dining on HarborWalk. For the best harbor sunset view, Boshamps is the local pick. Dewey Destin's at the harbor is where locals go for the freshest catch at the best price.

Where can I see fireworks in Destin in 2026?

The 4th of July fireworks over Destin Harbor are the biggest event. Best viewing spots are HarborWalk Village and from a Gulf-facing balcony at Pelican Beach Resort on US-98. The show typically starts at 9 PM and is visible for miles along the beach.

Is Henderson Beach State Park worth the entry fee?

Yes — at $4 per vehicle it is one of the best value beaches in Florida. The park has less than half the crowd of public beaches, pristine white sand, nature trails, and clean facilities. Arrive before 10 AM in peak season to guarantee a spot.

What is the cheapest way to get to Destin Florida?

Fly into Pensacola (PNS) or Panama City Beach (ECP) for the best fares — both are within 45–60 minutes of Destin. VPS is closest but has fewer routes and higher prices. Book 6–8 weeks ahead and fly midweek for the biggest savings.
