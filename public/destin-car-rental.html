Frequently Asked Questions
What is the closest airport to Destin Florida?
VPS (Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport) is the closest at 17 miles and ~25 minutes from Pelican Beach Resort. All major rental agencies are inside the terminal — no shuttle needed.
Should I fly into VPS, ECP, or PNS for Destin?
VPS is closest at 25 minutes. ECP (Panama City Beach) is 50 miles east — good for budget carrier fares. PNS (Pensacola) is 60 miles west with the most airlines and often lowest fares. Always compare all three before booking.
Do I need a rental car in Destin Florida?
Yes — Destin has no meaningful public transit. A car is essential for restaurants, groceries, and activities. Even staying beachfront at Pelican Beach you'll need one for anything beyond the beach.
How do I find the cheapest car rental at VPS?
Use DiscoverCars to compare all agencies — Avis, Budget, Alamo, Enterprise, National — in one search with transparent all-in pricing. Book at least 3-4 weeks ahead for summer dates as July inventory sells out fast.
Can I save money flying into a different airport?
Yes — ECP and PNS can save $50–$150+ per ticket vs VPS. For a family of four that's $200–$600 in flight savings. Factor in the extra 45-minute drive and rental car time when comparing total cost.
