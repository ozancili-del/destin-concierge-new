3 airports near Destin
VPS Destin–Fort Walton Beach Closest
17 miles from Pelican Beach Resort · ~25 min drive on US-98 East. Small terminal, quick pickup. Most convenient option.
ECP Panama City Beach
50 miles east · ~1 hr drive. Sometimes cheaper fares, especially on budget carriers. Worth checking.
PNS Pensacola Larger Hub
60 miles west · ~1 hr drive. Biggest airport in the region — more flight options and often lower fares.
💡 Pro tip

Search all three airports and compare. VPS is closest, but ECP and PNS can save you $50–$150+ per ticket — easily worth the extra drive.
