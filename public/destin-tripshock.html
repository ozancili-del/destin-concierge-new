Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best things to do in Destin Florida?

The top activities include Crab Island pontoon rentals, dolphin cruises from HarborWalk Village, deep sea fishing charters, jet ski rentals, parasailing, paddleboarding, and sunset sailing cruises.

What is Crab Island in Destin Florida?

Crab Island is a shallow sandbar in Destin Harbor and the #1 Destin experience. Rent a pontoon boat and anchor at the sandbar — the water is shallow enough to stand in, perfect for families.

Are there dolphin cruises in Destin Florida?

Yes — sunset and daytime dolphin watching cruises depart from HarborWalk Village. Most tours last 1-2 hours and dolphin sightings are very common in the Gulf of Mexico.
