Flights & Car Rental to Destin FL

For most guests staying at Pelican Beach Resort, VPS is the easiest airport because it is closest to Destin and the rental car counters are inside the terminal. Still, families should compare VPS, ECP and PNS because fare differences can sometimes outweigh the extra drive.

A rental car is strongly recommended in Destin. Restaurants, groceries, activities, Crab Island departures and HarborWalk are spread along US-98, and public transit is limited.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the closest airport to Destin Florida?

VPS, Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport, is the closest at about 17 miles and roughly 25 minutes from Pelican Beach Resort.

Should I fly into VPS, ECP, or PNS for Destin?

VPS is closest and easiest. ECP and PNS are farther but can sometimes offer cheaper fares or better schedules. Always compare all three before booking.

Do I need a rental car in Destin Florida?

Yes. Destin has limited public transit, and a car is useful for restaurants, groceries, activities and airport transfers.

How do I find the cheapest car rental at VPS?

Use a comparison tool like DiscoverCars, compare all agencies, and book early for summer dates.
